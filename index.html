What is a tree surgeon?

A tree surgeon is a specialist in the care and maintenance of trees. They are trained in the science of arboriculture and are focused on the health and wellbeing of trees. Tree surgeons can provide a range of services such as tree pruning, tree removal, tree planting, and tree health assessments.

How much does a tree surgeon cost?

There is no one answer to this question as the cost of a tree surgeon can vary depending on a number of factors, such as the size and condition of the tree, the location, and the type of services required. Contact us for a free no obligation quote.

Are you insured to be tree surgeons?

The team at Glasgow Tree Services are fully qualified arboriculturists. We have comprehensive public liability insurance covering up to £5m.

Is a tree surgeon the same as an arborist?

A tree surgeon is a term used interchangeably with arborist, however there is a slight difference in that a tree surgeon is someone who has had specific training in the care and surgery of trees, while an arborist is someone who simply works with trees.
